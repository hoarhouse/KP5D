How it works

Four steps from scattered conversations to connected knowledge.

01

Capture

The Chrome extension silently captures conversations from ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini as you work. SHA-256 deduplication means nothing is ever stored twice. Bulk import from official platform data exports handles your full history. No manual work required.

02

Index

The Tauri desktop app writes every conversation as markdown with YAML frontmatter into a local vault on your machine. SQLite FTS5 delivers sub-second full-text search across tens of thousands of messages. Nothing leaves your device.

03

Extract

The knowledge engine parses every conversation for entities, decisions, tools, and relationships. These become searchable, connected nodes in a local knowledge graph that spans every platform you've used. The graph gets smarter with every conversation.

04

Use

Search anything across all platforms. Transfer context to a new conversation in any LLM. Share clean, formatted snippets to Slack or email. Ask questions that span months of conversations. Weekly digests surface what matters before you even know to ask.
