Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Kept is a local-first application. This privacy policy explains how Kept handles your data. The short version: your data stays on your machine. We do not collect it, store it, or sell it.

What Kept does with your data

Kept captures AI conversations from ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok, and Kimi and stores them locally on your computer as SQLite database entries and Markdown files. This data never leaves your machine. There is no Kept server that receives your conversations, no cloud sync, and no third-party storage involved in the core product.

API keys

If you use Pro features that require AI inference (Smart Recall, knowledge graph extraction), Kept uses a bring-your-own-key (BYOK) model. Your API keys are stored locally on your machine and are sent directly to your chosen provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, OpenRouter, or a local Ollama instance). Kept does not proxy or log these requests.

Analytics and tracking

Kept does not include any analytics, tracking pixels, session recording, or behavioral monitoring. We do not know how you use the app.

Waitlist

If you join the waitlist, your email address is collected via Beehiiv solely for the purpose of sending you beta launch communications. You can unsubscribe at any time. We do not share your email with third parties.

Open source

Kept is MIT licensed and open source. You can inspect exactly what the application does with your data at any time by reviewing the source code on GitHub.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to how Kept handles data, we will update this page and note the date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to the E-Group Labs team via the GitHub repository.
