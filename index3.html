The structural advantage

They'll never build this
for each other.

ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok, and Kimi are all adding memory. Inside their own walls. These are competing companies. They will never index each other's conversations. That gap is permanent, and it widens every time a new model launches.

Netflix won't show you what's on Disney+. Amazon Prime won't surface your HBO Max watchlist. That's why JustWatch exists — it sits above all of them and tells you where everything lives. The same structural gap now exists for AI platforms, and no regulation is coming to fix it. Kept sits above all of them with Chat Archival, Conversation Forks, Context Templating, and Dynamic Links.

How it works

Four steps from conversation to connected knowledge.

01
Capture
Chrome extension detects your AI conversations automatically and saves them locally. Type @kept in any chat to capture it in one click, or let auto-sync run in the background. Save a full chat, just the last few messages, or a specific branch. No duplicates — Kept tracks what is already saved. Also supports bulk import from platform data exports for your full history.
02
Index
Tauri backend writes markdown with YAML frontmatter to a local vault. SQLite FTS5 delivers instant full-text search across thousands of conversations. Everything stays on your machine.
03
Extract
Knowledge engine parses every conversation for entities, decisions, and concepts. These become nodes in a local 3D knowledge graph mapping relationships across all your interactions, regardless of platform. Arcs automatically group related conversations into themes so connected ideas surface without any manual tagging.
04
Use
Ask questions about your past conversations using built-in Smart Recall. Semantic search finds relevant context even when you do not use the exact words. Frequently accessed knowledge surfaces automatically, and outdated facts fade over time. Share formatted snippets with your team, or carry context into any platform.
Core features

Four features.
Built for how developers actually work.

All four are free, open source, and available at beta launch. No account required.

01 / Chat Archival
"I forgot which model wrote this code."
Download and manage your AI conversations in a single desktop application. Every conversation from ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini lives in one searchable local archive. SQLite FTS5 search across thousands of messages in milliseconds.
02 / Conversation Forks
Let Claude finish what you started with Gemini.
Fork any conversation to a different AI platform without re-explaining. Kept packages the full context and opens a new thread in your chosen LLM with everything already loaded. Start task execution with multiple providers at the same time.
03 / Context Templating
Define it once. Stop pasting it forever.
Define exactly which files and context belong to the feature you're shipping. The Kept CLI watches for changes and keeps templates current. The extension injects the right context automatically into your AI conversations on every platform.
04 / Dynamic Links
Connect information across separate chats.
Link related conversations to build a knowledge graph that shows how your projects and ideas evolve over time. See how a decision made in ChatGPT connects to an architecture discussion in Claude weeks later.
